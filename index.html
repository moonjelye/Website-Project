Experience one of New Zealand's most breathtaking geothermal wonders…
With stunning geysers, bubbling mud pools, and breathtaking native bush, our personalized guided tours invite you to explore every corner of Te Puia.
Here, you’ll find the New Zealand Māori Arts and Crafts Institute, alongside a modern Kiwi Conservation Centre, giving you the chance to get up close to New Zealand's beloved national treasure—the native Kiwi bird.
For generations, Te Puia has opened its doors to manuhiri (visitors) from around the globe, inviting them to uncover the enchanting secrets of Te Whakarewarewa Thermal Valley, accessible both day and night. Nau mai, haere mai. We eagerly await your visit!
